Introduction

Our cafe was founded in 2022. Our sunny team will serve you quickly and easily.

We set up a café along the river bank, the idea started when many people sit on the river bank in Turku in the summer enjoying the wonderful weather.

In summer we have a terrace open. Live music opportunity.

Order cakes and treats from us for a party!

We offer delicacies and accept orders to your liking.
